Loan Data Exploration¶

Data and Investigation Overview¶

Here, I explore a dataset containing over 600 loan application decision as well as loan application attributes per applicant.In this investigation, I wanted to look at some attributes of the portfolio of these loan applicants that could be used to predict whether or not the loan application would be approved. The main focus was:

  1. Gender of the applicant
  2. Number of Dependents the applicants have - if they have any
  3. The education level of the applicants
  4. Whether or not the applicant is Self-Employed
  5. Whether or not the applicant has a credit history
  6. The neighborhood the person lives in

I think the dataset is clean enough for exploration

Data Exploration¶

Data Structure¶

There are 613 loan applicants in the dataset with 12 features characterizing each of them. There are -- numeric variables while the rest are qualitative. Of the qualitative variables, 4 are ordered. At this moment, I don't know if these are ordered from worst to best or vice versa cos I have no idea which variables are very vital to loan application decisions.

Variables of Interest¶

I am interested in figuring out what features/variables are best for predicting the decision of a loan application - Yes or No. I expect that Credit_History, LoanAmount, Loan_Amount_Term, CoapplicantIncome, ApplicantIncome, TotalIncome, Education, Property Area would have the most effect on the decision. However, this is just a hypothesis that must be validated

The credit history is also correlated with the loan status, as discovered earlier. This means that people with credit history are likelier to get loans than people without loans. Some correlation between dependents number and married. This is logical because married people are likely to have more dependents.
